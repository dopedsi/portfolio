About me

Formerly the Lead Designer for an EdTech company based in Melbourne, Australia, my main responsibility was to improve their Learning Management System design through lean and agile methodologies.

I enjoy identifying the problems users face through research and solve these issues to improve the end-to-end experience, creating a digital transformation full of satisfaction for both internal and external stakeholders.

Areas of specialisation include project management, analysing quantitative research for friction points, building design systems with WCAG accessibility in mind, and rapidly delivering MVPs that adhere to HCI principles.

Naturally curious, I am constantly finding new things to learn - whether it's delving into code so I can understand and communicate better with the development team or exploring 3D modelling and AR technology to future-proof my skillset.
