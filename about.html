About me

A UX designer/budding UX developer with over 3 years of experience working in various industries from education, beauty, insurance and project management.

I enjoy identifying the problems users face through research and solving these issues to improve the end-to-end experience, creating a digital transformation full of satisfaction for both internal and external stakeholders.

Areas of specialisation include project management, analysing quantitative research for friction points, building design systems with WCAG accessibility in mind, and rapidly delivering MVPs that adhere to HCI principles.

Naturally curious, I am constantly finding new things to learn - whether it's delving into code so I can understand and communicate better with the development team or exploring 3D modelling and AR technology to future-proof my skillset.

Ideally, I'd love the next step in my career to be towards bleeding edge technologies such as web3 and DAOs, but also gaming as I have a strong interest in these industries.
